CollectiveGovernance

Collective Governance implementation
Governance contract implementation for Collective. This contract implements voting by groups of pooled voters, open voting or based on membership, such as class members who hold a specific ERC-721 token in their wallet. Creating a Vote is a three step process First, propose the vote. Next, Configure the vote. Finally, open the vote. Voting may proceed according to the conditions established during configuration.
